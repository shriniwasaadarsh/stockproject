Current systems face challenges in accuracy, particularly in complex environments, leading to frequent misinterpretations of commands. The work delves into an innovative approach to address these challenges, introducing a reliable assessment scheme that leverages confidence scores from DeepSpeech and Tensor Flow, coupled with contextual clues from a Natural Language Processing (NLP) stage. The aim is to enhance the accuracy and reliability of voice command recognition systems used in micro air vehicle (MAV) control.
